# LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

## Objective

This repository contains research on how successful B2B SaaS founders use LinkedIn and long-form content to build their personal brands, educate audiences, and support business growth.

## Topic Chosen

**LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS**

## Research Scope

I researched the following founders:

- Jason Lemkin
- Adam Robinson
- Amos Bar-Joseph
- David Chevalier
- Dave Gerhardt
- Marc Lou
- Aaron Levie
- Girish Mathrubootham
- Paras Chopra
- Abhinav Asthana

## Repository Structure

- `research/sources.md` – List of selected experts and why they were chosen.
- `research/linkedin-posts/` – Summaries and insights from LinkedIn posts.
- `research/youtube-transcripts/` – Summaries and key takeaways from interviews and videos.
- `research/other/observations.md` – Cross-founder observations and recurring patterns.

## Why These Experts?

These founders consistently publish valuable content around SaaS growth, AI, go-to-market strategy, sales, product development, and founder-led marketing. Their public content provides practical insights into how LinkedIn can be used as an organic growth engine for B2B SaaS companies.

## Key Findings

- Educational content performs better than promotional content.
- Founder visibility increases trust and brand credibility.
- AI is being used to improve productivity, not replace strategic thinking.
- Authentic storytelling and practical insights drive engagement.
- Consistency and long-term thinking are critical for organic growth.
