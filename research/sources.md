## Research Method

For each expert, I will:
- Review recent LinkedIn posts and summarize key ideas.
- Review YouTube talks, interviews, or podcasts and capture important takeaways.
- Identify recurring themes and best practices for founder-led LinkedIn content in B2B SaaS.

## Goal

The objective of this research is to understand how successful B2B SaaS founders use LinkedIn to build authority, engage audiences, and drive business growth through consistent, high-value content.
