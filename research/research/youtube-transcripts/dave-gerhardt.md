## Summary

In this discussion, Dave Gerhardt and LinkedIn VP of Marketing Davang Shah explore how B2B marketing is evolving in response to changing buyer behavior and AI adoption. They explain that marketers are increasingly expected to demonstrate measurable business impact while also investing in long-term brand building. The conversation highlights the effectiveness of thought leader ads and founder-led content, which consistently outperform traditional corporate advertising by leveraging authenticity and expertise. They also emphasize that successful marketing requires looking beyond last-click attribution and understanding the full customer journey. Finally, while AI can improve efficiency in content creation and campaign optimization, human creativity, storytelling, and trust remain essential for building lasting customer relationships.

## Key Takeaways

- Thought leader content and founder-led advertising often outperform traditional brand advertisements by creating authenticity and trust.
- Brand building and performance marketing should work together rather than being treated as separate strategies.
- Measuring B2B marketing success requires tracking the entire buyer journey instead of relying solely on last-click attribution.
- Creative storytelling and human-centered messaging have a significant impact on campaign effectiveness.
- AI is a valuable tool for improving efficiency, but strategic thinking and genuine human connection remain critical for long-term success.
