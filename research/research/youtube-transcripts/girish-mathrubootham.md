## Summary

In this conversation, Girish Mathrubootham discusses how artificial intelligence is creating a once-in-a-generation opportunity for Indian startups to become global technology leaders. He explains that AI-native companies can scale much faster than traditional SaaS businesses by rethinking products and workflows from the ground up. Through his work at Together Fund, Girish emphasizes investing in founders who automate human-intensive tasks and build solutions for large, underserved markets. He also highlights the importance of founder mentorship, global exposure, and staying connected to innovation hubs such as Silicon Valley. Reflecting on Freshworks’ own AI journey, he concludes that the future belongs to businesses that redesign operations around AI rather than simply adding AI features to existing systems.

## Key Takeaways

- AI presents a significant opportunity for startups to create entirely new product categories and scale rapidly.
- Building AI-native businesses requires reimagining workflows instead of layering AI onto existing software.
- Automating human-intensive tasks can unlock massive market opportunities and create long-term business value.
- Founder support, operational expertise, and global market exposure are important drivers of startup success.
- Companies that proactively embrace AI transformation are better positioned to remain competitive in the future.
