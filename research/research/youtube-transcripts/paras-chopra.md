## Summary

In this interview, Paras Chopra shares his perspective on entrepreneurship, AI, and the future of technology innovation in India. Reflecting on his experience bootstrapping Wingify, he explains the value of building businesses with long-term thinking rather than focusing on short-term growth targets or external funding. Paras also discusses the importance of maintaining global quality standards, investing in original AI research, and exploring unconventional ideas instead of simply following existing trends. As AI continues to automate many cognitive tasks, he believes that qualities such as resilience, curiosity, and adaptability will become increasingly important for professionals. Throughout the conversation, he encourages engineers and founders to pursue side projects, think independently, and focus on continuous learning.

## Key Takeaways

- Bootstrapping can provide founders with greater independence and encourage sustainable, long-term decision-making.
- Competing globally requires maintaining high standards of quality and attention to detail from the very beginning.
- India’s AI ecosystem has significant potential if it invests in original research and differentiated innovation rather than imitation.
- As AI commoditizes technical skills, personal qualities like curiosity, resilience, and adaptability become key competitive advantages.
- Side projects and continuous experimentation help individuals develop new ideas, stay motivated, and build long-term expertise.
