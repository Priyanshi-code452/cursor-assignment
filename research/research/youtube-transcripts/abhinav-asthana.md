# Abhinav Asthana

## Video Title
Abhinav Asthana (Postman): APIs Are Eating the Agent Stack

## Video Link
https://youtu.be/AEI2ozuAXcw?si=zx6u5Uz1-tmpUpuz

## Summary

In this fireside chat, Postman CEO Abhinav Asthana explains how APIs are becoming the backbone of the emerging AI agent ecosystem. He discusses Postman’s transformation into an agent-native platform that supports both developers and autonomous AI systems through structured API catalogs and governance tools. Asthana highlights the growing importance of the Model Context Protocol (MCP) for enabling secure communication between AI agents and enterprise systems. He also describes how Postman has reorganized its teams around AI-assisted development, allowing faster product iteration and delivery. The conversation concludes with the introduction of Astro, a platform for managing AI agents, and the future potential of simulations for safely testing AI-driven workflows before deployment.

## Key Takeaways

- APIs will play a critical role in enabling AI agents to interact reliably with enterprise software.
- Governance, security, and discoverability are essential for deploying AI agents at scale.
- AI-assisted development is changing how product teams collaborate and ship software.
- Standards like the Model Context Protocol (MCP) can improve interoperability between AI systems and business data.
- Testing AI agents in simulated environments can reduce risks and improve reliability before production deployment.
