# Amos Bar-Joseph

## Video Title
Amos Bar-Joseph: This SaaS CEO is scaling to $10M ARR per FTE using AI Agents & Founder-Led Content

## Video Link

## Summary

In this interview, Amos Bar-Joseph explains how Swan AI achieved rapid early growth by relying on a founder-led go-to-market strategy instead of a traditional sales team. He describes building a strong personal brand on LinkedIn by consistently sharing insights, challenging conventional thinking, and documenting the company’s journey. Rather than positioning AI as a replacement for employees, he advocates for human-AI collaboration that enhances productivity and enables businesses to scale more efficiently. The discussion also highlights Swan AI’s internal automation systems, which use AI agents to manage customer engagement and streamline lead generation. Overall, the conversation demonstrates how authentic content, a differentiated narrative, and intelligent automation can work together to drive sustainable B2B SaaS growth.

## Key Takeaways

- Founder-led content on LinkedIn can become a powerful and cost-effective growth engine for B2B SaaS companies.
- Challenging industry assumptions with a unique perspective helps brands stand out and attract attention.
- AI is most effective when it augments human capabilities rather than replacing people entirely.
- Consistently sharing experiences and insights builds credibility, trust, and audience engagement.
- Internal AI automation can streamline lead generation and operational workflows while supporting scalable growth.
